10 Tracks / 1 Album

A concept album is loosely defined as an album whose tracks deal with a particular theme or idea.

In this section of Creative Computing, you’ll create a concept album that explores a theme or idea of your choosing by making use of HTML, CSS, and Javascript to give form to those ideas. Each week you’ll develop a track that incorporates newly learned techniques discussed in class. In the final weeks of the semester you’ll refine and sequence your tracks and develop any additional materials necessary to complete the vision for your album.

Below are a few concept albums that showcase a range of output—some of which stretch well beyond the audio produced for the album—that further explores the themes and ideas present in the albums.

The Beatles – Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band

Kanye West – My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy

Beyoncé – Lemonade

Frank Ocean – Endless / Boys Don't Cry / Blonde
