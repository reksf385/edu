Project 1

Create a concept album that explores a theme or idea of your choosing by making use of HTML, CSS, and Javascript to give form to those ideas. Each week you’ll develop a track that incorporates newly learned techniques discussed in class.

Project 2

In Methods & Practices you’ll be designing a portfolio website. During the second half of the semester you’ll apply the development skills learned in class to prototype ideas and incrementally build towards the final design of your portfolio site.
