Street Market

Street Market is a platform for selling sneakers on the secondary market.

  • Anyone can be a seller, anyone can be a buyer
  • Sellers ship product directly to consumer
  • Street Market takes 5% of sale + 2.5% transaction fee

Problem

In the last quarter, customer complaints of receiving fake shoes rose from 5.3% to 27.9%.

Task

Brainstorm design solutions that reduces the number of complaints we receive.
